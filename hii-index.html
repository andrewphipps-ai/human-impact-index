Living Resource

The Human Impact Index is a living research tool that tracks AI's transformation of the workforce through a human-centred lens. Where most industry metrics measure productivity gains, cost reductions, and automation efficiency, this index asks a different set of questions: who bears the cost of this transition, are affected workers receiving adequate support, and which communities face the greatest structural disruption?

Each region is analysed at both the regional overview level and through individual country profiles, enabling comparison across regulatory environments, labour market structures, and policy responses. The tool now covers the UK, North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific, with sector-by-sector momentum scores calibrated to reflect the pace and depth of AI-driven workforce change in each specific context.

This is not a forecast of job losses. It is an evidence-based assessment of transformation pressure, designed to support policy-makers, business leaders, and researchers in understanding where human impact is greatest and where intervention is most needed.
