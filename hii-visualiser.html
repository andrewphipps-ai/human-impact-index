Data & methodology

Scores are composite indices triangulated from OECD, ILO, ONS, Eurostat, and national statistical agencies. Each dimension is normalised to a 0–100 scale where higher values indicate greater risk or challenge to workers. Workforce scale modifier uses absolute employment data from ILO 2024 estimates.

Geographic concentration of impact
Where within each country are affected workers clustered?

Disruption is not evenly distributed within countries. This panel maps intra-national concentration — identifying the regions, cities, and communities bearing the highest share of workforce displacement risk, with implications for local labour markets and the commercial real estate assets that serve them.
